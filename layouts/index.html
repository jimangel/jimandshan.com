Resort Accommodations

The resort is called Grand Bahia Principe in Riviera Maya. There are 4 sub resorts which are all connected and have a cart system running to drive you to different areas. The different resorts are called Akumal, Tulum, Coba, and Sian Ka’an.

Our recommendation would be to book your room in either Akumal or Tulum as that is where most of the wedding related action will be. Jim and Shannon will be staying in the Akumal section, the ceremony will be held in the Tulum section, and the reception will be in Akumal. The difference between Akumal and Tulum is that Akumal has a private beach area and a few exclusive restaurants not available for use by Tulum guests, while Akumal guests have full access to the amenities in Tulum.

There is not a block of rooms so you can book through any avenue you would like. Booking directly through the resort website was the best price that we could find. http://www.bahia-principe.com/en/resorts-in-riviera-maya/resort-akumal/.

Coba and Sian Ka’an are the other locations available at the resort. Coba is located further back from the Ocean. Sian Ka’an is adults only and is across the 307 highway. It is connected to the other locations by the cart system, but is far from the ocean and other resorts.

You are more than welcome to stay off the resort!

Getting there

You will fly into Cancun International Airport. We booked our airfare through Southwest Airlines which is where we found the lowest price. It is a 1 hour and 15 minute drive to the Grand Bahia Principe. Booking your room through the resort website includes a complimentary shuttle to and from the airport (if you book elsewhere this may still be included, but double check!). The shuttle is a shared service with other travellers and does stop at multiple resorts making it more time consuming. If you are interested in a private transfer we have used http://www.usa-transfers.com/ in the past with good luck.
